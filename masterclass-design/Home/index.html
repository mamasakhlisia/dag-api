About This Masterclass

This intensive 3-day program is designed for dental professionals who want to elevate their implantology skills to the highest level. Through a combination of lectures, live demonstrations, and hands-on workshops, you'll gain the confidence and competence to handle even the most challenging implant cases.

The course covers the entire implant workflow from treatment planning to final restoration, with special emphasis on advanced bone grafting techniques, immediate loading protocols, and management of complications. You'll learn from leading experts who have placed thousands of implants and developed innovative techniques now used worldwide.

Who Should Attend

This masterclass is ideal for general dentists with basic implant experience who want to expand their skills, as well as specialists looking to refine their techniques. Participants should have placed at least 10-15 implants prior to attending.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced treatment planning for complex implant cases
  • Bone grafting techniques for predictable ridge augmentation
  • Immediate implant placement and loading protocols
  • Management of peri-implant soft tissues for optimal aesthetics
  • Hands-on practice with the latest implant systems
  • Complication prevention and management strategies
  • Digital workflows for implant planning and guided surgery
  • Practice management tips for growing your implant services
